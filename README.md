# Marriage Rate Analysis (1990-2016)

## 7COM1079 - Team Research Group B142
University of Hertfordshire

## Research Question
Is there a statistically significant correlation between year and marriage rates in U.S. states?

## Hypotheses
- **H0:** There is no correlation between year and marriage rates in U.S. states
- **H1:** There is a correlation (positive or negative) between year and marriage rates
